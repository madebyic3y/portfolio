About

Beatrice Danica C. Blando, also known as Icey, is a Multimedia Arts student with a deep passion for creativity, visual storytelling, and design. She specializes in graphic design, photography, video editing, and content creation, often blending traditional arts and crafts with digital media.

Driven by curiosity and imagination, Icey explores various areas of the multimedia field—from creating handcrafted visuals to experimenting with new design tools and techniques. Her work reflects a love for detail, color, and meaningful storytelling, whether through still images or moving visuals.

As she continues to grow in her craft, Icey remains committed to learning, improving, and expressing ideas through different artistic forms, transforming concepts into compelling, heartfelt visuals.
